Times like this, we need stories. Personally, I like a nice heartwarming story about a girl and her horse. Or when kids get in over their heads and take on a little more than we think they're capable of. Those are nice stories and they make me feel good. But feeling good doesn't get you out of trouble. More often than not, it let's you ignore the trouble until it's knocking on your door. Even then, it can be tempting to ignore it. Times like this we need a story about a person that's just trying to keep his head down and be a worker among workers. You see this guy and you see he seems a little meek, but you've seen stories like this and your gut tells you that he's just trying not to do something that'll have consequences beyond his control.
