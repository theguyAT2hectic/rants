Counter culture attracts a specific personality type — creative, dynamic, character driven, instinctively rejecting received authority
The early 90s alt scene as proof — not one genre but one ethos cutting across punk, industrial, goth, folk, synth
The 100 record collection argument — Pretty Hate Machine next to Flood next to Disintegration next to Never Mind the Bollocks. Not random, coherent
DIY as the connective tissue — Ani DiFranco, Wax Trax, Ministry, all operating outside the system simultaneously
The record store and the venue as institutions that transmitted values through friction and curation
Crash-n-Burn specifically — the smell, the selection, the guy behind the counter with actual skin in the game
The D&D nerds are worker bees, the counter culture kids become the creators and leaders — people who evaluate individuals not products
